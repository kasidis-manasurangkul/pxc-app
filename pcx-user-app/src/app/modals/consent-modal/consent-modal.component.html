Consent and Privacy Notice

Welcome to our platform. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and internationally recognized privacy and security standards.

This notice explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By continuing to use our services, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Username (required)
  • Interaction Data: Books you have rated or added to your wishlist
  • Preference Data: Genre selections made explicitly
  • No sensitive personal data (e.g., national ID, health data) is collected

2. Purpose of Data Use

  • To generate personalized book recommendations based on your preferences and usage history
  • To enable platform features such as wishlists, personalized libraries, and rating history
  • To improve system performance, stability, and usability
  • To comply with legal and security obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a summary of the personal data we store about you through your user dashboard
  • Deletion: Delete your account at any time; all associated data will be deleted or pseudonymized unless required by law

4. Data Integrity & Reliability

  • All user input is validated to meet defined boundaries
  • Changes to personal data are logged for integrity
  • Data is backed up periodically and restorations are tested

5. Privacy by Design & Compliance

  • Internal logs use pseudonymized identifiers
  • Data minimization is applied to all features
  • PDPA compliance includes breach protocols, retention policies, and user rights enforcement

By clicking “Accept and Continue”, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this privacy notice and agree to the collection, processing, and usage of your data as described.

If you do not wish to provide this consent, you may click “Decline” and discontinue use of the platform.
